Common Questions

Kitchen Renovation FAQs — Ottawa

How much does a kitchen renovation cost in Ottawa?

Kitchen renovation costs in Ottawa vary widely based on scope and materials. A focused refresh — new cabinets, countertops, and backsplash — typically runs $20,000–$35,000. A mid-range full renovation with custom cabinetry, quartz countertops, and new flooring runs $40,000–$65,000. A high-end gut renovation with premium finishes, custom millwork, and appliances can reach $80,000–$120,000+. We provide detailed, transparent quotes so you know exactly what you're getting.

How long does a kitchen renovation take in Ottawa?

Most kitchen renovations in Ottawa take 3–6 weeks from demolition to completion. A simple cabinet and countertop refresh can be done in 2 weeks, while a full gut renovation with new layout, plumbing, electrical, and custom cabinetry typically takes 4–8 weeks. We provide a clear timeline upfront and communicate any changes throughout the process.

Do I need a permit for a kitchen renovation in Ottawa?

Most kitchen renovations — new cabinets, countertops, flooring, backsplash, and fixture replacements — do not require a permit in Ottawa. However, if your project involves moving plumbing, adding or relocating electrical panels or circuits, or removing load-bearing walls, permits will be required. We assess your project upfront and advise you on any permit requirements.

Can I stay in my home during a kitchen renovation?

Yes — most Ottawa homeowners stay in their homes during kitchen renovations. We set up a temporary kitchen area where possible and minimize the time your kitchen is completely non-functional. We clean up thoroughly each day so your home remains comfortable. For major gut renovations, we discuss the timeline and disruption with you upfront so there are no surprises.

Do you do open concept kitchen renovations in Ottawa?

Yes — open concept kitchen conversions are one of the most popular renovations we do in Ottawa. We assess whether walls are load-bearing, handle any required structural work including beams and posts, and complete the full conversion including patching floors, ceilings, and walls so everything blends seamlessly. It's one of the best ways to modernize an Ottawa home.

Do you renovate kitchens in Kanata, Barrhaven, and surrounding Ottawa areas?

Absolutely — we complete kitchen renovations throughout Ottawa and the surrounding region including Kanata, Barrhaven, Manotick, Stittsville, South Ottawa, Rockland, Wendover, Casselman, Arnprior, Almonte, and Carleton Place. We're Ottawa-based and the majority of our kitchen renovation projects are right here in the Ottawa area.
