Who We Are

Not a Corporation.
A Family.

TNA Pro Build is a family-owned renovation company rooted in Ottawa. When you hire us, you're not getting a call centre or a project manager who subcontracts everything out. You're getting the people who will actually show up, roll up their sleeves, and build something they're proud of.

We started with a simple belief: homeowners deserve honest tradespeople who communicate clearly, respect their space, and deliver what they promised. That hasn't changed.

"Quality. Care. Trust." — these aren't just words on our business card. They're how we've built our reputation, one project at a time.

From a custom cedar sauna in Barrhaven to a full kitchen reno in Kanata — every project gets the same attention we'd give our own home.
