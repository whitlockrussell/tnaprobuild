About Our Work

Ottawa Deck, Fence & Porch Builders

Ottawa homeowners know that a well-built deck, fence, or porch doesn't just look good — it adds real square footage to your living space and serious value to your property. At TNA Pro Build, we've been building custom outdoor structures across Ottawa, Barrhaven, Kanata, Manotick, Stittsville, and surrounding communities, and we understand what it takes to build something that lasts in our climate.

Ottawa's freeze-thaw cycle is one of the harshest on outdoor structures in Canada. That's why we don't cut corners on footings, materials, or fasteners. Every deck we build is engineered to handle the weight of Ottawa snow loads and the expansion and contraction that comes with our temperature swings. We use premium cedar and trusted composite brands — nothing that will warp, rot, or pull apart after two seasons.

Whether you're looking for a simple backyard deck to enjoy summer evenings, a multi-level entertainment platform with built-in seating and planters, a front porch that adds character to your home's curb appeal, or a privacy fence to define your outdoor space — TNA Pro Build delivers quality craftsmanship at a fair price. Deck and fence projects in Ottawa typically range from $8,000 for a basic deck to $40,000+ for a large multi-level build with custom features.

As a family-owned Ottawa company, we take pride in every project we put our name on. You deal directly with us — no subcontractors, no middlemen — just an honest team that shows up on time and builds things right.

Common Questions

Deck, Fence & Porch FAQs — Ottawa

How much does a deck cost in Ottawa?

Deck costs in Ottawa typically range from $8,000–$15,000 for a basic ground-level cedar deck, $15,000–$30,000 for a larger or elevated deck with railings and stairs, and $30,000–$50,000+ for a multi-level deck with custom features like built-in seating, planters, and pergolas. Composite decking adds roughly 20–30% to the cost of cedar. We provide detailed quotes based on your specific project.

Do I need a permit for a deck in Ottawa?

In Ottawa, a building permit is generally required for decks that are attached to the house, more than 24 inches above grade, or larger than 108 square feet. We're familiar with Ottawa's permit requirements and can advise you on whether your project needs one and how to get it. We always build to Ottawa building code standards.

How long does it take to build a deck in Ottawa?

Most deck builds in Ottawa take 1–2 weeks from start to finish, depending on size and complexity. A basic ground-level deck can sometimes be completed in 3–5 days, while a large multi-level deck with custom features may take 2–3 weeks. We provide a clear timeline upfront and work efficiently to minimize disruption to your yard and home.

What's the best decking material for Ottawa's climate?

For Ottawa's climate, we recommend pressure-treated lumber for the structural frame regardless of your surface material choice. For decking surfaces, cedar is a popular and beautiful choice that performs well in our freeze-thaw cycle when properly maintained. Composite decking is an excellent low-maintenance alternative that won't need staining each season. We'll help you choose the right material for your budget and lifestyle.

How much does a fence cost in Ottawa?

Wood fence installation in Ottawa typically costs $30–$60 per linear foot installed, depending on the style and height. A standard backyard privacy fence for an average Ottawa lot runs $3,000–$8,000. We provide free quotes based on your property and the fence style you're looking for.

Do you build porches in Ottawa?

Yes — we build front porches, back porches, covered porches, and screened porches across Ottawa and surrounding areas. A front porch is one of the best curb appeal upgrades you can make to an Ottawa home. We design porches to match your home's existing architecture and build them to the same standard as all our outdoor structures.
