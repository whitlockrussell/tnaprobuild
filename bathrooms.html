Common Questions

Bathroom Renovation FAQs — Ottawa

How much does a bathroom renovation cost in Ottawa?

Bathroom renovation costs in Ottawa typically range from $12,000–$20,000 for a standard 3-piece bathroom refresh, $25,000–$50,000 for a full ensuite renovation, and $60,000–$100,000+ for a luxury spa-style bathroom with a cedar sauna, freestanding tub, and custom tile work. The final cost depends on the size of the space, materials chosen, and scope of work. We provide detailed, transparent quotes before any work begins.

How long does a bathroom renovation take in Ottawa?

A standard bathroom renovation in Ottawa typically takes 2–4 weeks from demolition to completion. Larger, more complex renovations — like a full ensuite with custom tile, glass shower, and sauna — can take 4–8 weeks. We provide a clear timeline upfront and communicate any changes throughout the process.

Do you handle permits for bathroom renovations in Ottawa?

For most bathroom renovations — tile, fixtures, vanities, showers — permits are not required in Ottawa. However, if your project involves moving plumbing, adding electrical circuits, or structural changes, we'll advise you on permit requirements and can assist with the process. We ensure all work meets Ottawa building code standards.

Can I stay in my home during a bathroom renovation?

Yes — in most cases you can stay in your home during a bathroom renovation. If it's your only bathroom, we work efficiently to minimize downtime and ensure you always have access to essential facilities. We clean up each day before we leave so your home stays comfortable throughout the project.

Do you renovate bathrooms in Barrhaven, Kanata, and surrounding Ottawa areas?

Yes — we serve all of Ottawa and the surrounding region including Barrhaven, Kanata, Kanata North, Kanata South, Manotick, Stittsville, Carp, South Ottawa, Rockland, Wendover, Casselman, Arnprior, Almonte, and Carleton Place. We're Ottawa-based and most of our bathroom renovation projects are right here in the Ottawa area.

What bathroom renovation services do you specialize in?

TNA Pro Build specializes in full bathroom gut renovations, custom tile and stonework, frameless glass showers, freestanding soaker tub installations, floating vanities, custom cedar saunas, heated floors, and complete ensuite builds. We handle all trades in-house — tile, plumbing fixtures, electrical, and finishing — so you deal with one team from start to finish.
