Common Questions

Basement Finishing FAQs — Ottawa

How much does it cost to finish a basement in Ottawa?

Basement finishing costs in Ottawa typically range from $30,000–$45,000 for a basic open-concept finish with flooring, drywall, pot lights, and paint. A mid-range basement with a rec room, wet bar, and bathroom addition runs $50,000–$70,000. A fully custom basement with high-end finishes, a home theatre, gym, and full bathroom can reach $80,000–$120,000+. We provide detailed, transparent quotes so you know exactly what you're getting before work begins.

How long does it take to finish a basement in Ottawa?

Most basement finishing projects in Ottawa take 4–8 weeks from start to completion. A basic open-concept finish can be done in 3–4 weeks, while a fully custom basement with a bathroom addition, wet bar, and home theatre may take 8–12 weeks. We provide a clear timeline upfront and keep you informed throughout the process.

Do I need a permit to finish my basement in Ottawa?

Yes — in Ottawa, a building permit is required for basement finishing projects that include framing new walls, adding electrical circuits, adding plumbing, or creating a new bedroom or bathroom. We're familiar with Ottawa's permit requirements and can advise you on what's needed for your specific project. We always build to Ottawa building code standards.

How do you prevent moisture problems in Ottawa basements?

Moisture management is critical in Ottawa basements given our climate. We use moisture-resistant insulation, proper vapour barriers, and moisture-tolerant framing materials where appropriate. We assess your existing waterproofing situation before we start and advise you on any issues that should be addressed before finishing. A properly built basement is a dry basement.

Can you add a bathroom to my Ottawa basement?

Yes — adding a bathroom to a basement is one of our most popular services. We handle the full rough-in plumbing, framing, tile, fixtures, and finishing. Most Ottawa homes have the rough-in already in place, which makes the process straightforward. If your home doesn't have a basement rough-in, we can assess the feasibility and provide a quote for the full installation.

Do you finish basements in Kanata, Barrhaven, and surrounding Ottawa areas?

Absolutely — we complete basement finishing projects throughout Ottawa and the surrounding region including Kanata, Barrhaven, Manotick, Stittsville, South Ottawa, Rockland, Wendover, Casselman, Arnprior, Almonte, and Carleton Place. We're Ottawa-based and most of our basement projects are right here in the Ottawa area.
